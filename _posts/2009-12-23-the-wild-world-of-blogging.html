I've never really considered the whole idea of blogging.  I mean all that comes to mind when I hear "blogging," is telling others about your day, the bull you believe in, and how just about nobody cares.  I don't actually feel this blog will be any different, but I feel my reasoning is a bit different than what others go into blogging for.  I feel like this will be a good way for me to witness how the whole process works, and allow it to give me ideas and showcase different achievements in life.  Whether or not this blog will actually amount to anything I'm entirely unsure, but I will be able to handcraft my own words without concern for the structure of my page or the programming that it will take to create it.  Though I am eternally interested in how that works.

Let us see how the whole damn thing turns out.
