Tomorrow I graduate. Well, not for real.  I'll walk at my graduation ceremony, but I won't technically know if I've graduated college until about a month from now.   I can't say that I'm incredibly excited about the whole thing.  Sure, I'll be doing without homework and right after I'm done I'll be heading off to the wild world of east asia, but I'm mostly indifferent.  I think most of this has to do with how much work is still to be done in the next three days and then how much traveling will need to be done after that. Well I finally get to Taiwan I'll be happy, until I realize that I'm completely lost in every respect then it might take a bit more to get used to.  Until then I need to look forward to my graduation, despite all of the work that needs to be done until then and in conjunction.
