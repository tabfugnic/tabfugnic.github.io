I had a post for this ready that I was typing up on the plane, but I consumed four glasses of wine before writing.  It was not only largely incoherent, but also very boring.  So instead I'm going to write a new one that will be equally as boring, but I will be completely sober.

That flight was long.  So so long. Sixteen hours worth of just sitting in the middle seat of the middle section of a plane.  I'm a pretty big guy and thankfully these planes have more than the average domestic flight, but it was still cramped. I did manage to get up a few times and stretch.  Worst of all, I had a preteen girl on the left of me in an aisle seat that clearly didn't need much leg room and kept on falling asleep on me.  The one time I tried to pass the old woman on the right of me, she reminded me that the little girl was smaller and therefore easier to get by and I should just take that route each time I needed to get up.

Like any international flight, nothing really exciting happened.  I watched a bunch of movies and I was far too uncomfortable to even try to read something.  I didn't sleep the entire time, though I definitely tried and right now I've been up for somewhere in the ball park of 35 hours.  Though I might be exaggerating.  Either way I'm tired.  Still a bit nervous too.  This will be, as I've said before, the first time I've ever gone to a country by myself and on top of that I'm going to be staying with people I've never met that speak little to no English.  It should definitely teach me how to understand and speak mandarin a whooole lot more.

Until then, I anxiously await my flight.
